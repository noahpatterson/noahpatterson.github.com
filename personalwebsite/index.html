WMATA Nextbus Bus Tracker Redesign

In this project I imagined and protyped how the DC bus tracker website could be improved.The buses in this protopye move through different zones on the page which indicate at a quick glance how long you have to get to the bus. Green signifies you have plenty of time, yellow signifies you should leave to catch the bus and red signifies you will likely miss the bus.
